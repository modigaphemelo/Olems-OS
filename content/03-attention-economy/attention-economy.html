03. Attention Economy

Reclaiming your focus capital in an age of engineered distraction

Your attention is the processor time of your life.
Every app, person, notification, and thought competes for cycles.
The modern world is engineered to extract this capital from you.
This series is about becoming system administrator of your own attention.

The Core Crisis

We've moved from an Attention Economy (where attention is traded) to an Addiction Economy (where attention is farmed). The algorithms are designed by neuroscientists to maximize engagement, not your wellbeing. Your willpower is a flickering candle against industrial-strength psychological engineering.

The CPU Analogy

Your attention is CPU time. Every notification is an interrupt request. Social media is a memory leak. This series teaches you to allocate processor cycles intentionally, block attention malware, and optimize your cognitive architecture.
