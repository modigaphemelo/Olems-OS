Resource allocation that compounds. From memory leaks to compounding returns

The difference between renting processor time

The Full Cup Principle

You cannot serve from an empty cup. Society condemns "selfishness" while simultaneously draining you for its needs. Personal Economics is the strategic selfishness of building such profound personal capacity that your overflow becomes your contribution. What looks like selfishness is actually systems-level responsibility.

The Portfolio Analogy

Your life is an investment portfolio. Most people invest their resources in depreciating assets (distraction, drama, depletion). This series teaches you to invest in compounding assets (skills, relationships, health, capital) that pay dividends for decades.
