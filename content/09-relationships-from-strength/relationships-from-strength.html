How to share your data without compromising your security.

The Network Security Principle

Most relationship problems come from poor boundary architecture: either completely open (vulnerable to exploits) or completely closed (isolated and lonely). Relationships From Strength is about designing intelligent connection protocols: establishing secure channels, defining access levels, monitoring traffic, and maintaining your core security while still sharing value.

The Network Analogy

Your relationships are like a computer network. Some people get SSH access (deep trust), some get API access (functional collaboration), some only get website access (public information), and some get blocked at the firewall (toxic). This series teaches you to design and manage this network intelligently.
